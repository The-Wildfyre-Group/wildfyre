BUSINESS MATTERS


“ Marathoning 101: Set Milestones, Know Your Pace ”

It’s easy to lose time. I envision it tip-toeing away each hour, before finally sneaking out in the middle of the day with a stick and bag. Business management is like a marathon, it’s important to set milestones and stick to the schedule. Each missed deadline for one item creates a ripple effect of missed deadlines for additional items. Know the milestones, know your pace.


“ Free Throws: Don't Miss the Point ”

I can explain this best with a quote I love. “Customers don’t go to the hardware store because they want a quarter-inch drill bit. They go because they need a quarter-inch hole. Many startups miss this point, focusing on features of the drill that don’t matter to the customer. “ -Jason Fried


“ Grand opening, grand closing. ”

Companies don’t become successful by closing a sale once. Companies (and people) become successful when they become magicians. They figure out how to magically close a sale while simultaneously opening another; as magicians, they also figure out how to earn repeat business, build loyalty, and pull rabbits out of hats. A sale is never closed.


“ Even a product that sells itself needs to be discovered. ”

Let’s not kid ourselves, we all desire to create a product that “sells itself.” The issue is some people foolishly believe that a product that sells itself doesn’t require marketing. “Even a product that sells itself needs to be discovered.” Some level of marketing is always required. Find your target audience.


“ Finish fast ”

The most important thing is to finish fast. The quality of a finished product can be improved.





PERSONAL GROWTH

“ Wake Up ”

I despise dreamers. Let me rephrase that, I despise dreamers that only dream. It’s expectedly exciting to have amazing dreams when you’re sleeping, but realize this: A dream will never be realized until you ‘Wake Up’ and start the pursuit.


“ Go Long ”

In finance, a ‘long position’ is buying an asset with the expectation that it will rise in value. In a popular hedge fund strategy, activist investing, the investor gains control of the target company and makes decisions on company’s behalf. This strategy works because the company is making an investment where they have the most control. There is no investment where you are more in control than investing in yourself. Go long.


“ Sleep ”

Unlike wake up, this isn’t a metaphor. This is literal. As much as we try to operate on 4-5 hours of sleep per night, it’s unhealthy and the fatigue will reveal itself in one form or another. This is the area where I need the most improvement going forward.


“ Remove Distractions ”

Technology gives us the ability to invite distraction. Working on the computer gives us the ability to fire up our Twitter, Facebook, GroupMe, Instagram, etc. and always be plugged in. Maximize productivity by removing distractions for the entire workday or set blocks.
