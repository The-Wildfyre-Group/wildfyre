BUSINESS MATTERS


“ Take breaks or breaks will take you... ”

It's real simple. If you don't allow the mind, body, and spirt to break and run free at regular intervals you will experience a BREAK DOWN. Which is a systematic failure where your internal programming overrides your deadlines, priorities, and other worldly cares and you are shown who is the boss.


“ Add two weeks... ”

No matter how conservative, how likely, how feasible, or how certain you are that a thing will be done by a certain time - "add two weeks". Life happens and in the world of business an open week can quickly turn into a action-packed 96-hour whirlwind that leaves you wondering - "where did the days go?". Whether it's the expected or the unexpected that thwarts your innocent 'target date', be safe, be smart....add two weeks. It will most often still leave you behind schedule, but at least you won't look like a complete a**...

“ Cross that bridge when you get there... ”

There are so many things for us to argue and debate, let's at least make sure we're having an exchange that actually has an ACTIONABLE OUTCOME to be opinionated about. You'd be surprised how many conversations fall quiet when a simple question is asked - "Do we need to have this conversation now?"....be sure to ask that question often before posturing on a matter that doesn't really matter...








PERSONAL GROWTH

“ So much of life is self-esteem... ”

I was in Dallas, TX in a beyond beat up infinity that literally felt like it was coughing when my Cousin said this to me. He had no money, his car was beyond beat up, no girl, but his walk was still strong and his confidence to right all of his wrongs on 100. Confidence is really everything.


“ You ain't had no problems till you had crack cocaine problems... ”

Be blessed. Especially if you are fortunate enough to have "uptown problems". The problems of the world will rarely confront you unless you seek them out. People are hungry, at war, completely impoverished, uneducated, and there are no clinics to check into. What "problems" do you really have?
