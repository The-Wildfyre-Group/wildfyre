Kcoa



Khadijah Coakley is an architect. She designs the structure around ideas. She started out as a banker and somewhere along the line converted to a passion-driven creative. She is hungry for details and enlightenment. She has always wanted to be better at math and feels embarrassed while calculating tips with her iphone. She specializes in overdoing everything and is easily the most humbly-arrogant woman you have ever met. Yes, she made up 'humbly-arrogant' and she'd like you to start using it.
