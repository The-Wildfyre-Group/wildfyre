HOW I GOT HERE / WHERE I HOPE TO GO / MOTIVATING FORCES

Advise early-stage companies in areas of fundraising, corporate structuring, revenue viability, research-driven concept validation, niche product development, and market positioning. Act as lead designer and front-end developer specializing in information architecture.



HOW I GOT HERE...


I studied economics at Berkeley. Loved it. Pursued 'Banking' for all the same reasons. 2 years later I left for all the same reasons. I was fortunate to work in an incredibly diverse environment with some of the smartest and most thoughtful individuals I didn't know could co-exist in a corporate setting. Then I left. Somewhere in the middle I realized there was only three things I loved -- Design, Technology, and Health & Fitness. It wasn't enough to have them as hobbies. I had this concept of "I'd Rather Be...". It was the mental inefficency and pre-occupation of what you'd rather doing at any point in time. It was a mental check, and I wasn't passing. I had a vision of asking myself this question, filling in the blank and being exactly where I wanted to be doing exactly what I wanted to be doing...and I ended up here.
